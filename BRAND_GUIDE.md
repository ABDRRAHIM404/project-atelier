# بيتي بذوقي — Brand UI Guide

The supplied brand artwork is the visual source of truth for V1.

## Accessible UI tokens

- Primary teal: `#247B79` — primary buttons, links, active states.
- Dark teal: `#155B59` — hover states and high-emphasis text.
- Gold accent: `#9B7624` — focus rings and restrained highlights; not used for body text on light surfaces.
- Ink: `#243735` — headings and primary text.
- Muted text: `#5F6F6B` — secondary text with accessible contrast.
- Ivory background: `#FBFAF4`.
- Warm surface: `#F2EDDF`.
- Border: `#D8CEB5`.

## UX rules

- Teal remains the only primary action color to preserve hierarchy.
- Gold is decorative/accent-only because the brighter logo gold has insufficient contrast on white.
- The logo retains its original proportions and is never stretched.
- Decorative brand imagery does not replace page headings or actionable labels.
- Focus indicators remain visible for keyboard users.
- Header navigation and CTA remain readable and responsive from 320px upward.
